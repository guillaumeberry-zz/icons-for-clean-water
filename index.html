An icon design project

1 out of 11 people in our world is drinking water that comes from a river, lake or muddy hole in the ground every day. Dirty, contaminated water that has leeches and debris in it. Water that causes disease, keeps kids out of school and takes hours to bring back home.

I joined charity: water’s September Campaign this year because I believe that every person on the planet deserves clean water, and I’m looking for anyone who can help me.

See campaign

I'm designing at least 38 icons as a contribution. The first 20 People giving something to this campaign get to choose one metaphor or object for me to design. The icons will be available online for free and for all by the end of this campaign (end of December).

Thanks for your support to this cause.
